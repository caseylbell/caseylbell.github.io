Welcome

Hello, CST 201! This website includes pages that serve as tutorials for all Adobe-centered labs taught in this undergraduate CSUMB course. When you navigate to each page and complete the provided tutorials, you will learn the following:

  • Demonstrate your ability to work with different layers and mask in Adobe Photoshop.

  • Demonstrate your ability to create custom shapes and use the pen tool in Adobe Illustrator.

  • Demonstrate your ability to work with Master Pages and use the Desktop Publishing feature in Adobe InDesign.

  • Demonstrate your ability to use Adobe Color to create a color blind safe & high contrast color palette.

  • Demonstrate your ability to create GIFs using Adobe Animate.
