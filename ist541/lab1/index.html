How To Use The Photoshop Clone Stamp To Take A Person/Object Out Of An Image

By Casey Bell


Step 1: Open The Image

Open your image in Adobe Photoshop.

Step 2: Duplicate The Base Layer

Right click on the Background layer (or press "Command+J" on your Mac keyboard), then select "Duplicate Layer", to create a new layer above the Background layer.

Step 4: Use The Clone Stamp

To use the clone stamp, do the following:

  1. Press "Option" on your Mac keyboard, and hold down this key while you click a desired portion of the image that will successfully replace part of the person.
