Pen Tool


Step 1: Open Adobe Illustrator & Name Your Project

Open Adobe Illustrator. Then, name your Adobe Illustrator project "pen_tool.ai" and place your images into your project (while ensuring the images stay proportional with one another).

Step 2: Set Images Side By Side

Set your images next to each other:

Step 3: Change Opacity Of Images

Change the Opacity of all images to 25%:

Step 4: Select The Pen Tool

Ensure your Pen Tool is selected and your Stroke is set to black & Fill is set to no fill:

Step 5: Trace The Images

Trace over each image with the pen tool.

Step 6: Delete The Images

Once you trace the images, you can delete the images, so you only see your artwork:

Step 7: Customize Your Artwork

Go to "Window" in the top menu, then select "Brushes"; now, you can select your artwork and change the brush type as you desire; you can also change the color or stroke of your artwork within your Properties panel, as usual:

Step 8: Export Your Icons As Selections

Go to "File" in the top menu, then select "Export Selection"; now, you can select each of your icons and export them individually as PNGs.
